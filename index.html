Encompass Supply Chain Solutions — Shipping Rules
Encompass Shipping Cutoff
⏰ Time-sensitive
📞 (800) 432-8542 ⏱ Cutoff: 2:00 PM Eastern / 1:00 PM Central
Standard / Regular Shipping
Does NOT ship same day — ever. Ships the following business day regardless of when the order is placed. Never use Standard if the tech needs the part for a next-day callback.
2nd Day Air or Next Day Air
Ships same day only if ordered before 1:00 PM Central. After 1:00 PM Central — order sits until next business day even with expedited shipping selected.
Weekend Orders
FedEx treats Encompass as closed on weekends. Any order placed Friday after 1:00 PM Central, or Saturday/Sunday, ships Monday regardless of shipping method selected.
⚡ The rule that matters on a live call
If a tech needs a part for a next-day callback, the order must be placed before 1:00 PM Central with 2nd Day Air or Next Day Air selected. Standard shipping after 1:00 PM Central means the part will not move until the following business day at the earliest.

Whirlpool Control Board Recall (Part Ordering)
WhirlpoolRecallW11766193
⚠ Service Pointer
📞 Whirlpool Parts: 1-800-952-2537 (option for Parts and Services)
For the recall issue of a failed control board causing a constantly running drain pump — covered under Technical Service Pointer #W11766193 Rev C.
When to call it in
Customer is OK waiting on a possible backorder of the boards, AND the unit is a new unit made within the pointer's serial number range.
Top Load Washers
Models ranging from CC01xxxx to CD05xxxx
Stacked Laundry Units
Models ranging from MC01xxxx to MD05xxxx
📋 How to call in the order
1. Call Parts Dept 1-800-952-2537 (Parts and Services). 2. Provide model #, serial #, date of closing (purchase date), and part #. 3. Confirm the serial is still covered under the pointer (W11766193 Rev C) — if so provide customer number, name, and address. 4. Get the reference number and ETA. If backordered, tell the customer they can call Whirlpool Parts to follow up after 14 days. 5. Double-check the shipping address. 6. Add all info to the Parts Ordered List.
Follow-up calls
Customers to call back. Standard check-ins at 2 days. Quality checks for complex or second-trip jobs.
