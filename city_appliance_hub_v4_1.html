Encompass Supply Chain Solutions — Shipping Rules
Encompass Shipping Cutoff
⏰ Time-sensitive
📞 (800) 432-8542 ⏱ Cutoff: 2:00 PM Eastern / 1:00 PM Central
Standard / Regular Shipping
Does NOT ship same day — ever. Ships the following business day regardless of when the order is placed. Never use Standard if the tech needs the part for a next-day callback.
2nd Day Air or Next Day Air
Ships same day only if ordered before 1:00 PM Central. After 1:00 PM Central — order sits until next business day even with expedited shipping selected.
Weekend Orders
FedEx treats Encompass as closed on weekends. Any order placed Friday after 1:00 PM Central, or Saturday/Sunday, ships Monday regardless of shipping method selected.
⚡ The rule that matters on a live call
If a tech needs a part for a next-day callback, the order must be placed before 1:00 PM Central with 2nd Day Air or Next Day Air selected. Standard shipping after 1:00 PM Central means the part will not move until the following business day at the earliest.
